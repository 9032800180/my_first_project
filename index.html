Problem

Students often struggle to identify weak areas and structure their learning efficiently. With scattered resources and limited guidance, progress can be slow and untracked.

Solution

SkillGrow uses AI to assess skills, generate a personalized learning roadmap, and recommend targeted resources. Collaborate with peers, track progress, and iterate as you grow.
